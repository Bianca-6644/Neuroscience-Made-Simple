What we do

We are a group of students passionate about neuroscience. We notice neuroscience is a field lacking opportunities for students to get involved in. We hope to inspire more people to get involve with neuroscience by creating content about neuroscience in a way that is easy for beginners to understand.

  • Learn More

How we spread awareness about neuroscience

  • Lessons

    We create contents in the form of powerpoints and infographics to make learning neuroscience an easy and fulfiling path.

  • Flashcards

    Based on our contents, we will make g=flashcards on Anki to let users review and remember what they learned in their long-term memory.

  • Research Papers

    Neuroscience field is constantly updating with new findings so it is essential we keep updated with the lastest news.
